I cannot easily be put into few words. I’m a teacher, a coder, a child and follower of God, and many others. Graduating from my hometown high school was the start of my journey to impacting people all over. I have not achieved that yet, but I am getting closer each day. Navarro College helps me by giving me a place to do basics, and make friends and contacts. I’ll head on to UNT to get a Bachelor’s of Science in Interdisciplinary Studies. While I have not planned exactly what I will do after UNT, I know I want to teach middle schoolers. I have no doubt that I will affect the world by teaching. Teaching allows me to share knowledge to many people who will go in various directions as they age. I can also better the world with programming. From simple throw-away programs to big projects, each line of code can do something big; something amazing.

That is a small fragment of me. I am much more, and my journey is not over: It is just beginning!
